*   **CRISPR-Cas9 is a revolutionary gene editing tool:** Derived from a bacterial defense system, it allows precise DNA cutting at targeted locations, transforming research and applications.
*   **CRISPR has broad applications and impact:** It's used in agriculture (disease resistance), medicine (e.g., sickle cell therapy), and basic research (gene function), with significant commercial investment.
*   **Ethical concerns, especially germline editing, remain:** The potential for heritable changes raises significant ethical debates and necessitates regulation, highlighted by the He Jiankui controversy.
