Let's solve the logic puzzle step-by-step using the clues:

---

### **Clues Recap:**

1. **Bob is not at either end.**  
   → So Bob is in positions 2, 3, or 4.

2. **Alice is somewhere to the left of Dave.**  
   → Alice’s position < Dave’s position.

3. **Carol is immediately next to Bob.**  
   → Carol is adjacent to Bob (either to his left or right).

4. **Eve is at one of the ends.**  
   → Eve is in position 1 or 5.

5. **There is exactly one person between Alice and Carol.**  
   → The distance between Alice and Carol is 2 (i.e., positions differ by 2).

---

Let’s consider the **five positions** as:

**[1] [2] [3] [4] [5]**

We'll try to **assign people** to these positions using the clues.

---

### Step 1: Use **Clue 4 (Eve is at one of the ends)**

Eve is in **position 1 or 5**.

Try **Eve in position 1**.

---

### Step 2: Use **Clue 1 (Bob is not at either end)**

→ Bob is in **positions 2, 3, or 4**

---

### Step 3: Use **Clue 3 (Carol is immediately next to Bob)**

So, if Bob is in position 2, Carol is in 1 or 3  
If Bob is in 3, Carol is in 2 or 4  
If Bob is in 4, Carol is in 3 or 5

But we already **assumed Eve is in position 1**, so **Carol can't be in position 1** (since that's Eve), unless we **change that assumption**.

Let’s **try Eve in position 5** instead (we'll come back to this if it fails).

---

### Try: **Eve in position 5**

Now, we can try assigning Bob to positions 2, 3, or 4.

---

### Try Bob in **position 2** → Carol must be in **position 1 or 3**

Try **Carol in position 3** (since 1 is open)

So far:

- [1] = ?
- [2] = Bob
- [3] = Carol
- [4] = ?
- [5] = Eve

Now, **Clue 5**: There is **exactly one person between Alice and Carol**

Carol is in position 3 → Alice must be in position **1** or **5**

But 5 is Eve, so Alice must be in **position 1**

Now we have:

- [1] = Alice
- [2] = Bob
- [3] = Carol
- [4] = ?
- [5] = Eve

Now check **Clue 2**: Alice is to the **left of Dave**

Alice is in position 1 → Dave must be in **positions 2 to 5**, but **position 2 is Bob, 3 is Carol, 5 is Eve**, so **Dave must be in position 4**

Now we have:

- [1] = Alice  
- [2] = Bob  
- [3] = Carol  
- [4] = Dave  
- [5] = Eve

Let’s check all clues:
