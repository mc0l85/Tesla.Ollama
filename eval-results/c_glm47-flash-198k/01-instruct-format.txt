1. Drinking water helps maintain the balance of body fluids that regulate digestion, absorption, and circulation.
2. It helps lubricate the joints and protect the spinal cord and other sensitive tissues.
3. Water helps regulate body temperature through sweating and respiration.
4. It helps maximize physical performance, particularly during intense exercise or high heat.
5. Drinking water can help prevent dehydration, which can cause fatigue and negatively impact mood.
